PTIK FKIP UNS

The Informatics and Computer Technology Education (PTIK) study program is planned to take shelter in the management of the Engineering and Vocational Education (PTK) department. This placement revises our previous statement, where the PTIK study program is under the P.MIPA department. PTIK is a study program that will

produce graduates who are prioritized to teach in vocational/vocational programs . So, it would be better if PTIK was under the PTK department which oversees vocational programs such as Building Engineering Education , and Mechanical Engineering Education. To meet the needs of lecturers in the field of information technology that cannot be met by the PTK department, PTIK will conduct resource sharing with the mathematics study program.
